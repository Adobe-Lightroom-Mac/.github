## Overview

Adobe Lightroom Advanced is a professional photo management and editing platform that combines a comprehensive catalog system with powerful non-destructive RAW editing tools, serving photographers who manage large libraries alongside demanding editing workflows.

The catalog system organizes photos with collections, smart collections, metadata, star ratings, color labels, and keyword tagging, making it practical to navigate and find specific images within collections of hundreds of thousands of files without losing any organizational work.

RAW editing with the latest Adobe Camera Raw engine processes files from all major camera manufacturers with the color science, noise reduction, and detail rendering that professional photographers rely on for consistent, high-quality output.

Masking tools powered by AI select subjects, skies, backgrounds, and objects with high precision, making complex local adjustments that previously required careful manual masking achievable in seconds with clean, accurate edges.

For photographers who manage large libraries and want the industry-standard editing and organization tools that integrate with the broader Adobe ecosystem, Adobe Lightroom Advanced is the photo production platform that professional photographers have standardized on for organizing and editing their work.

## Performance

Adobe Lightroom balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
